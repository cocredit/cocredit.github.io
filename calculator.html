Your borrowing capacity depends on your monthly trail income and the purpose of the loan. For acquisitions, you can typically access up to 70% of your book value. For working capital, you can access up to 50%. Use the calculator above to get an estimate based on your trail income.

The book multiple is used to estimate the value of your trail book. It typically ranges from 2x to 3.5x your annual trail income, depending on factors like the quality of your loan book, lender mix, and historical runoff rates. The default of 3x is a common industry benchmark.
