Common Questions

How is my trail book valued?

We look at several factors including monthly trail income, loan age, runoff rate, lender mix, and book growth. Our automated systems analyze your RCTIs to give you an accurate picture quickly.

What happens to my clients?

That depends on the buyer and deal structure. Some deals keep you managing the client relationships, others transition clients to the buyer. We'll explain all options.

How long does the sale process take?

From valuation to settlement, most deals complete in 45-60 days. Getting your initial valuation takes just 24 hours.

I'm not sure if I want to sell. Can I just get a valuation?

Absolutely. Many brokers get a valuation just to know their number - it helps with business planning, even if you don't sell right now. No obligation whatsoever.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. We don't share your details with anyone without your explicit permission. Your competitors, aggregator, or anyone else won't know you're exploring a sale.
