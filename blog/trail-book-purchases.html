The Secret to Scaling Your Mortgage Broking Business Through Trail Book Acquisitions

The secret's out, more Mortgage Brokers have unlocked the strategy to double (or even triple) their trail book over a 12-month period.

Until recently, trail book financing has been difficult to achieve unless the Broker's book is worth in excess of $3m. To date, its been a process which has been historically expensive and cumbersome.

With new fintech lenders appearing in the trail book lending space, buying trail books is no longer a strategy reserved for the top 2% of Mortgage Brokers. In the past, Trail book loans have been tough to implement, but also the cost of the debt has largely outweighed the benefit of the transaction.

Introducing Co.Credit: Your Specialized Trail Book Lender

Co.Credit is a specialised trail book lender, completely focused on the needs of the Broker, to help them build bigger businesses. As a specialised lender to Brokers, Co. is able to quickly and easily value any Broker's book in real time. The speedy process allows for an approval within 24 hours, giving Brokers confidence and clarity in their ability to secure funding.

How Trail Book Acquisitions Can Transform Your Business: A Practical Example

Let's break down a practical example:

  • A trail book generates $100k per annum
  • The book is being sold for 3x annual revenue: $300k
  • Co.Credit provides a $300k loan for the purchase
  • Monthly trail income: $8.3k
  • Loan repayments are less than the monthly trail income
  • Result: Positive cashflow and $300k added business value
